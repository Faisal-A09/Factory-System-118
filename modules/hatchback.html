REFLECTION SECTION · DEVICES & REFRIGERANTS

The air-conditioning and cooling units that serve the Hatchback area. Each card explains what the unit is, what it does, and which refrigerant it runs on. Colors below map each refrigerant to its units.

R-407 R-410 R-507 R-404 Freon-free · Galco
About the refrigerants. Each unit is tagged with the refrigerant it uses so technicians can match the right gas and tools to the right equipment during service. The FAU is the exception — it carries no Freon at all and instead air-cools using a flow of cold Galco. A full cross-section refrigerant map is available from the Refrigerant Index in the main Aqwat menu.
